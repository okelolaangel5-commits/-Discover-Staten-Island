History Timeline

Staten Island was first settled by the Dutch in 1624. It later became one of New York City's five boroughs in 1898 and played a major role in shipping, immigration, and culture.

Pop Culture

Staten Island is the birthplace of the legendary hip-hop group Wu-Tang Clan. It has also appeared in many films and series.

Politics

Staten Island has its own Borough President and often has different political views compared to the rest of NYC.
