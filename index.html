History

Staten Island was originally home to the Lenape Native Americans. It later became a Dutch settlement and officially joined New York City in 1898.

Politics

Staten Island has a unique political identity compared to other NYC boroughs, often voting differently and focusing on local governance.

Pop Culture

Home of the Wu-Tang Clan, Staten Island is strongly connected to hip-hop, film, and modern American culture.

Nature

It is the greenest borough in NYC, filled with parks, coastlines, and open spaces.
