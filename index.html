Fun Facts

The Staten Island Ferry is free.
It is the least populated NYC borough.
Freshkills Park was once the world’s largest landfill.
Staten Island has beaches and forests.

History

Staten Island was originally inhabited by the Lenape Native Americans. It was later colonized by the Dutch and became part of New York City in 1898.

Timeline

1609: First European exploration

1661: First settlement founded

1898: Becomes part of NYC

1964: Verrazzano-Narrows Bridge opens

Politics & Government

Staten Island is governed by New York City. It has a Borough President and representatives in the NYC Council. It is known for having more conservative voters compared to other boroughs.
