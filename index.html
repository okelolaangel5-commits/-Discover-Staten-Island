DISCOVER STATEN ISLAND

By Angel Okelola · Stella · Tila · Eske · Johan

History

Staten Island was originally home to the Lenape Native Americans. European exploration began in 1609, followed by Dutch and British rule. In 1898, Staten Island officially became the fifth borough of New York City. It developed more slowly than others, preserving green spaces and suburban life.

Pop Culture

Staten Island gained global recognition through the Wu-Tang Clan, one of the most influential hip-hop groups in history. The Staten Island Ferry frequently appears in films and TV shows and is an iconic symbol of New York.

Politics

Politically, Staten Island often differs from the rest of NYC. It has strong local identity, civic engagement, and representation through a borough president and city council members.
